Observed test-only categories:
- Remaining OrderBy/ToArray and several dictionary/list allocations occur in tests.
- FindStaleHandles, NeedsMonitorOverrideLookup, SelectFadeDurations, and related helpers are referenced only by test facades.

Optimization conclusion:
- No additional production hot-path allocation or native-work issue was justified by the final scan.
- Remaining production allocations are either necessary data materialization, already pre-sized, event/user-action driven, or UI binding work.
